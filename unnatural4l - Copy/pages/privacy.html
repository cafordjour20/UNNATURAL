UNNATURAL Privacy Policy

At UNNATURAL, your privacy is a top priority. We are committed to safeguarding the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website and services. By using our site, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

UNNATURAL is more than just a brand—it's a movement. Born from the belief that success is not defined by conventional paths, we aim to inspire individuals to break free from societal expectations and create their own destiny. Founded with the vision of empowering those who dare to be different, UNNATURAL represents resilience, individuality, and the courage to take the road less traveled. Every design we produce embodies this spirit, blending bold aesthetics with meaningful messages that encourage self-expression and authenticity.

How We Use Your Information

At UNNATURAL, we are committed to delivering high-quality apparel designed to empower and inspire. Every product is crafted with purpose—from the selection of premium materials to the creation of designs that reflect boldness, confidence, and individuality. Our vision is to be a brand that not only provides unique fashion but also fosters a community of like-minded individuals who embrace their true selves and refuse to conform.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand your preferences and optimize website functionality. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that certain features of the site may not function properly without them.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no online transmission or storage system is entirely secure. We encourage you to take precautions, such as using secure passwords and keeping your login details confidential.

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, and delivering products. These providers are contractually obligated to keep your data secure and use it only for the purposes specified by us.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or wish to request changes to your personal information, please contact us at contact@unnatural.com.
